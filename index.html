At ChallengePost, our mission is to celebrate and inspire hackers. That's why we're hosting 8 online hackathons where you can sharpen your skills, stretch your imagination, and most importantly, have fun. Each hackathon runs for 3 weeks and is open to hackers around the world.

June 29–July 20

Maps
as art

Powered by Clusterpoint

Reimagine the world around you and unleash your inner cartographer. Create a ‘wallworthy’ interactive map that illustrates one way in which you see the world.

June 29–July 27

Internet of
Things

Powered by Weaved

Does your watch know what your coffee maker is up to? Invent new applications, build on existing IoT platforms, or create your own. And don't worry about the singularity, we're sure everything will be 200 OK.

July 6–July 27

Your first
API

Powered by Apiary

GET. POST. CURL! APIs are the glue that bind apps, commerce, the IoT, and well… everything these days. Build your own API and connect an app, dataset, or service to the rest of the world.

July 6–July 27

The bionic
hacker

Powered by BaseHealth

Every day your smartphone collects TONS of data about you. Miles walked. Calories burned. Time spent reading Hacker News. We've got the technology, now harness that data and use it to make yourself better… faster… stronger!



June 1–22

Reinvent
the selfie

Powered by Twilio

Selfies have come a long way since MySpace & duck faces. I mean hello, we have selfie sticks now! But what's next? Drones? Custom bokeh? Satellite selfies?! Show us the next evolution in self-portrait tech.

June 1–22

Open data
mashup

Powered by Socrata

Around the world, governments, organizations, and corporations are getting hip to the #opendata movement. Mashup your favorite open data sets and teach us something new.

June 8–29

Financial literacy

Powered by Venmo

If you know more about the Kardashians than your bank account, this hackathon is for you. Financial literacy affects you, your livelihood, and your community. Build new tech & apps that help people learn about and take charge of their finances.

June 15–July 6

Connect with your team

Powered by Twitter

Ok sports fans, it's gametime! Whether you root for the Cubs or Team Liquid, you and your team are a community. Create new ways and apps to bring players, teams, and fans closer together.
